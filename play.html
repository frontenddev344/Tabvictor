Playing Guide

Our comprehensive guide will walk you through the exciting journey of playing fantasy sports on our app.

Getting Started

1.1 Download and Install

Visit the App Store or Google Play Store.

Search for "TabVictor" and download the app.

Install the app on your device.

1.2 Create an Account

Open the app and sign up using your email or social media account.

Complete the registration process by providing necessary details.

Navigating the App

2.1 Home Screen

Explore the home screen to find featured matches, ongoing leagues, and upcoming events.

Check out personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

2.2 Sports Lobby

Navigate to the sports lobby to choose from a variety of sports available on TabVictor.

Select your preferred sport to view available matches.

Choosing a Match

3.1 Browse Matches

Scroll through the list of matches for your chosen sport.

Check match details, teams, and player statistics.

3.2 Selecting a Contest

Click on a match to view available contests.

Choose a contest that suits your preferences, such as entry fee and prize pool.

Building Your Fantasy Team

4.1 Drafting Players

Use your virtual budget to draft a team of real players for the chosen match.

Pay attention to player positions, recent performances, and team dynamics.

4.2 Strategy and Selection

Make strategic choices based on player performance, matchups, and your sports knowledge.

Build a balanced team with a mix of star players and potential game-changers.

Joining a League

5.1 Public or Private Leagues

Choose between public leagues for random opponents or private leagues to compete with friends.

Join existing leagues or create your own for a customized experience.

5.2 League Settings

Check league settings, such as scoring rules and roster requirements, before joining.

Follow the Action

6.1 Real-Time Updates

Track your fantasy team's performance with real-time scoring updates during the live match.

Monitor player stats, milestones, and game-changing moments.

Celebrate Your Success

7.1 Claiming Prizes

If your fantasy team performs well, you could win exciting prizes.

Learn how to claim your winnings through the app.

You've now embarked on a thrilling fantasy sports journey with TabVictor. Our app is designed to provide an immersive and engaging experience for every sports enthusiast. Play smart, have fun, and may the fantasy gods be in your favour!
