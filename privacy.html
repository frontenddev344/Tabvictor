Privacy Policy for TabVictor

Welcome to TabVictor! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our fantasy sports app in New Zealand. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency in the handling of your data.

Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

During account creation, we collect your name, email address, and other necessary details.

When using our app, we may collect information about your device, location, and IP address.

1.2 Transaction Data

If you participate in paid contests, we may collect payment information securely through our payment processor.

1.3 User Content

Information you provide when participating in discussions, contests, or communicating with customer support.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Account Management

To create and manage your TabVictor account.

2.2 Game Participation

To enable your participation in fantasy sports contests, including team creation and scoring.

2.3 Communication

To respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and send important updates related to your account.

2.4 Analytics

To analyze user behavior, improve our services, and personalize your experience on TabVictor.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

3.1 Service Providers

Trusted third-party service providers assisting us in delivering our services.

3.2 Legal Compliance

When required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.

Your Choices and Rights

4.1 Account Settings

You can update your account settings and preferences at any time.

4.2 Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information.

4.3 Communication Preferences

Opt-out of promotional communications through your account settings.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience and analyze usage patterns. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. You will be notified of any significant changes, and the updated policy will be posted on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please contact us at info@tabvictor.com By using TabVictor, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Thank you for choosing TabVictor for your fantasy sports experience in New Zealand!
